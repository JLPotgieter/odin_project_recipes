Spaghetti

Description

Spaghetti is very tasty. Like guitars have strings that tickle the ears, spaghetti has strings that tickle the tastebuds.

Ingredients

  • Spaghetti noodles
  • Tomato sauce
  • Grated cheese

Steps

  1. Boil spaghetti noodles until tender.
  2. Heat tomato sauce in a pan.
  3. Combine noodles and sauce.
  4. Serve with grated cheese.
