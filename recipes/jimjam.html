Recipe for a JimJam

Description

A jam with Jimi or a 'JimJam', as it is known colloquially, is any form of creative activity engaged in under the auspices of Jimi. Jamming with Jimi is generally productive, yet relaxing. But one must make the most of it for as long as it's there, as there typically won't be a follow up, Jimi being a rather mercurial creature.

Ingredients

  • Musical instrumnt or singing voice
  • Crafts materials
  • Video camera
  • Creative energy (though Jimi has it in abundance)

Steps

  1. Go to Jimi
  2. Talk to Jimi
  3. Create with Jimi
  4. Bake until golden.
