Pizza recipe

Description

Pizza is a very tasty dish. Like cars have wheels that roll around, pizza is a wheel that wants to roll around in your belly.

Ingredients

  • Pizza dough
  • Tomato sauce
  • Mozzarella cheese
  • Toppings (e.g., pepperoni, vegetables)

Steps

  1. Preheat oven.
  2. Spread sauce on dough.
  3. Add cheese and toppings.
  4. Bake until golden.
